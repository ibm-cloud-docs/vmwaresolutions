# Ordering vRealize Operations and Log Insight
{: #vrops_ordering}

You can include the vRealize Operations™ and Log Insight™ service with a new VMware vCenter Server® instance or add the service to your existing instance.

## Ordering vRealize Operations and Log Insight for a new instance
{: #vrops_ordering-new}

1. When you [order the instance](/docs/vmwaresolutions?topic=vmwaresolutions-vc_orderinginstance-procedure), scroll down to the **Add-on services** section. vRealize Operations and Log Insight is in the **Management tools** category.
2. Open the category, locate vRealize Operations and Log Insight, and toggle its switch on.
3. Click **Edit** to review and specify the configuration information, then click **Save**.

## Ordering vRealize Operations and Log Insight for an existing instance
{: #vrops_ordering-existing}

1. On the instance details page, click the **Services** tab.
2. Click **Add** to add the service.
3. On the **Add services** page, locate the **vRealize Operations and Log Insight** service and toggle its switch on.
4. Click **Edit** to review and specify the configuration information, then click **Save**.
